Our Story

Delizisio is about friends, family and community. We are an independently-owned continental restaurant serving dishes made with seasonal ingredients sourced from local producers. We pride ourselves on our menu, service and passion for food. Our beautifully appointed dining room is the perfect setting for any occasion — an intimate, romantic dinner, or when you have reason for celebration and want all your closest friends together at the same table. We have updated our fabulous brunch, lunch and dinner menus! Make your reservation now and enjoy the best continental food all over the world. We are proud to announce that it was voted into the Top 10 Most Restaurants in the world for 2015. The award is based on feedback from you, our dear and valued customers. We are so happy to know that your experiences at Delizisio have been so memorable, and we will do all we can to make sure you enjoy dining with us every time you come in.
